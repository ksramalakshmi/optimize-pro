CSV Upload Guidelines & Documentation

Format Overview

Your CSV file must include a header row with specific column names to seamlessly map your sales history.

Required Columns

  • sku: The exact product SKU matching your catalog (e.g., WH-1000).
  • marketplace: The marketplace exact code (e.g., amazon, flipkart, meesho).
  • quantity: The number of units sold in this transaction. Must be a positive integer.
  • sale_price: The final selling price per unit. Must be numeric.

Optional Columns

  • date: The date of the sale in YYYY-MM-DD format. If omitted, the system defaults to today's date.

Do's and Don'ts

Do's ✅
  • Ensure SKUs precisely match the capitalization of products already added to your system.
  • Format dates as standard YYYY-MM-DD or standard datetime if including historical timelines.
  • Verify the marketplace codes are active in your settings.
Don'ts ❌
  • Don't include currency symbols in the `sale_price` column.
  • Don't leave the marketplace blank—we need to know where the stock originated.
